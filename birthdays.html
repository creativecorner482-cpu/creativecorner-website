Questions

Party FAQ

Do guests need experience?

No experience needed. Our instructor guides everyone step by step, so beginners are completely welcome.

Can we bring food and drinks?

Yes. You're welcome to bring food, drinks, cake, and decorations. We just ask that you clean up personal items after the party.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks ahead, especially for weekends. Popular dates fill up quickly.

Can we choose what we paint?

Absolutely. Let us know your theme or subject ideas when you book, and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Is there a deposit required?

Yes. A $100 deposit is due upon booking to reserve your date. The remaining balance is due the day of the event.

What is included?

Painting instruction, canvases, paints, brushes, and setup are included. Each guest takes home their finished artwork.

Ready to party?

Send us your preferred date, group size, and party type. We'll follow up with availability and next steps.
